Product Information

The Yukon Grizzly Locker is a mechanical locking differential built for serious off-roaders. It functions similarly to a ratchet wrench, locking both wheels together under load while allowing one wheel to overrun when necessary (like when cornering). However, it never lets a wheel spin slower than the drive speed ensuring maximum traction at all times.

The entire case and internals are forged from 8620 nickel-chromium-molybdenum tool steel, a premium material chosen for its exceptional strength and resistance to shock-loading. Whether you're rock crawling, mudding, or dealing with uneven terrain, this locker is designed to handle high torque and brutal impacts without failure.

What makes this locker even more appealing is its fully automatic operation with no cables, switches, or buttons. When you need traction, it engages. When you don’t, it disengages seamlessly, keeping your driving smooth and controlled.
